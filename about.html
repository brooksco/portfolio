Development Photography

I'm originally from Seattle, WA, and currently live in NYC. I am enrolled in the Art & Art Education MA program at Columbia University. I graduated in May 2013 from Grinnell College in Iowa with a degree in Computer Science, and a concentration in Neuroscience. I've spent my summers working with at-risk youth, doing art in the park with kids in Iowa, and building websites for community non-profits. I've previously spent time working with a youth sports tech company, as a mentor with Youth in Focus, and as a freelancer in Wellington, New Zealand.

I like a lot of different things, so my approach to projects reflects that.
