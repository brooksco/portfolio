About Development Photography

I'm originally from Seattle, WA, and currently live in New York. I'm enrolled in the Art & Art Education MA program at Columbia University, studying the intersection of arts and technology in education. I graduated in May 2013 from Grinnell College in Iowa with a BA in Computer Science, a concentration in Neuroscience, and an affinity for the rural Midwest.

My interests are pretty diverse, so I've ended up stradling a number of different disciplines over the years. I've spent time working with at-risk youth, doing art in the park with kids in Iowa, building websites for community non-profits, freelancing in New Zealand, and helping to teach teens traditional black and white photography, while largely sticking academically to hard sciences. Most recently I was working at a youth sports tech company, building team-managment and social web and mobile software.

I like a lot of different things, and particularly those that manage to mash together arts and science all at once. And space.
